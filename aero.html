Research in Aeronautics

Aircraft Fuselage Design

The fuselage is the backbone of an aircraft, housing critical components like the cockpit and passenger cabin. My research optimizes fuselage design for structural integrity and aerodynamics, leveraging composite materials to enhance fuel efficiency and performance.

Aero Engine Technologies

Aero engines power aviation's future. My work explores turbofan and turbojet engines, focusing on improving thrust-to-weight ratios and reducing emissions through computational modeling and innovative designs.

Avionics Systems

Avionics systems are the brain of modern aircraft, integrating navigation, communication, and flight control. My project develops interactive simulations of avionics interfaces, enhancing pilot situational awareness and system reliability.
